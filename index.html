Den Lukkede Afdeling bench trio, announce new CS2 lineup

After a rough streak of losses, the Danish squad reshuffles its starting five and hands over key roles to a new core.

by staff Dec 9, 2025 Updated 17:00 CET
ROSTER OVERVIEW
Team: Den Lukkede Afdeling
Game: Counter-Strike 2
Change: 3 benched · 3 role swaps
Status: Effective immediately

After a period characterized by too many losses and not enough highlights, the leadership of Den Lukkede Afdeling has decided to make significant changes to its CS2 starting lineup.

Effective immediately, @Deepsito, @Scharn9 and @taica will step out of the starting five and move to the bench. In their place, a new core will take over the key roles in the team.

New starting lineup & roles

@Marcus
Support
Utility, trading and dirty work in late rounds.
@Draygus
Entry fragger
First man in – first contact, first chaos.
@submastersubs
In-game leader (IGL)
Takes over calling duties from @Scharn9.
@Bentsen
AWPer
Steps into the sniper role previously held by @taica.
@Uldnesok
Anchor
Replaces @Deepsito as site anchor and defensive backbone.

The organisation clarifies that @Sok will still be part of the starting lineup when timing allows – especially on the days where, as the team jokingly states, “he is not sleeping, as he is the star of the team”.

Benched players

@taica @Deepsito @Scharn9

Even though the results have not lived up to expectations, the organisation underlines that all three benched players have been important in building the current identity of the team.

Thank you to the benched players

@taica – the team’s own m0NESY

The organisation thanks @taica for his mechanical skill ceiling and explosive impact. When he was on, he could take over rounds in a way reminiscent of the young sniper superstar. Even in the missed shots, there was creativity and the willingness to decide the game.

@Deepsito – the dupreeh-style workhorse

Described as the “glue” of the roster, @Deepsito often took on the sacrificial roles and difficult positions for the sake of the team. The organisation highlights his loyalty, work ethic and willingness to take duels no one else wanted to touch.

@Scharn9 – the gla1ve-like mastermind

As in-game leader, @Scharn9 has been the mind behind the structure, even in chaotic games. Although the results did not always match the ideas, the organisation emphasises his contribution to culture, tactics and long-term thinking in the squad.

Decision from the top of the organisation

According to the statement, the roster move comes directly from the top level of the organisation, “Den Lukkede Afdeling”. The decision is presented as a necessary step to create clarity in roles, responsibility and future direction.

“This change is not about pointing fingers, but about giving the team a fresh start. We are grateful for what the benched players have contributed and believe this new structure is the right move for the next chapter,” the organisation states.

Players who wish to seek a new organisation or get a direct explanation of the changes are encouraged to reach out either to the upper management of Den Lukkede Afdeling, or to @Uldnesok, who is described as being closest to the leadership and having “competent leadership skills” in the internal announcement.

With the new roster locked in, attention now turns to whether Den Lukkede Afdeling can turn their recent slump around and stabilise their performance in upcoming matches.
